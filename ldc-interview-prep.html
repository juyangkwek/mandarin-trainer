PREP 2难句拆解Three sentences from the article, chunked
Same method as the Lesson 4 stumble clinic, applied upward: three sentences a band above your level, dismantled so the machinery shows. Master the chunks and the register becomes reachable.
这些环环相扣，协助集团确保基本粮食和农业供应链的运转。
zhèxiē huánhuán xiāngkòu, xiézhù jítuán quèbǎo jīběn liángshi hé nóngyè gōngyìngliàn de yùnzhuǎn
原文 (transcribed from image — verify). Why it matters: this is your 一体化 argument in native executive idiom.
这些环环相扣zhèxiē huánhuán xiāngkòu"these interlock, link by link" — 环 (ring) doubled, 相扣 (mutually fastened). The image your dash-and-connect sentences have been reaching for.
协助集团xiézhù jítuán"helps the group" — 协助 is the formal 帮助; you know 集团 cold.
确保quèbǎo"to ensure" — 确 (certain) + 保 (protect). A word your risk-management decade will use constantly.
基本粮食和农业供应链jīběn liángshi hé nóngyè gōngyìngliàn"the basic food and agricultural supply chain" — stacked modifiers, big-to-small as ever.
的运转de yùnzhuǎn"’s operation/running" — 运转 = to keep turning; what the whole sentence exists to protect.
把产品从世界各地的农民那里，运送到终端消费者手中。
bǎ chǎnpǐn cóng shìjiè gèdì de nóngmín nàli, yùnsòng dào zhōngduān xiāofèizhě shǒu zhōng
原文 (transcribed — verify). Why it matters: a full 把 + 从…到… sentence — the grammar you attempted in Lesson 4’s 把-construction, at journalism grade.
把产品bǎ chǎnpǐn把 grabs the product — same construction as your 我们把…连在一起.
从世界各地的农民那里cóng…nóngmín nàli"from farmers all over the world" — 那里 turns people into a place you can move goods from.
运送到yùnsòng dào"transport to" — verb + 到 lands the movement at its destination.
终端消费者手中zhōngduān xiāofèizhě shǒu zhōng"into the hands of end consumers" — 手中 (in the hands of) is the elegant close; farm to table, literally farmer to hand.
从教师大胆转换跑道，在农业大宗商品交易大展拳脚。
cóng jiàoshī dàdǎn zhuǎnhuàn pǎodào, zài nóngyè dàzōng shāngpǐn jiāoyì dà zhǎn quánjiǎo
The section headline (adapted). Why it matters: this is the career-arc template — swap the nouns and it’s your story.
从教师cóng jiàoshī"from (being) a teacher" — the starting lane. Yours: 从管理顾问.
大胆转换跑道dàdǎn zhuǎnhuàn pǎodào"boldly switched tracks" — 大胆 (big-gutted) admires the leap; 跑道 is the running lane.
在农业大宗商品交易zài nóngyè dàzōng shāngpǐn jiāoyì"in agri-commodities trading" — note 交易 here as the field of dealing; your scripts still say 贸易 for the flow.
大展拳脚dà zhǎn quánjiǎo"spread fists and feet wide" — made his mark. The idiom a profile writer would one day hang on you.
PREP 3模拟采访Mock media interview — the kit
The format is plausible reality: Zaobao interviewing Wilmar’s sugar head is a story their business desk would run. Six questions in the journalist’s register for Queenie to fire at you cold — your existing three dialogues answer Q1–Q3 nearly verbatim; Q4–Q6 are the gap this module exists to close.

1请您先介绍一下自己和您的工作。Qǐng nín xiān jièshào yíxià zìjǐ hé nín de gōngzuò.Dialogue 1, as is. Note 您 — expect it, return it.

3可以具体讲一讲你们在不同国家的业务吗？Kěyǐ jùtǐ jiǎng yi jiǎng nǐmen zài bùtóng guójiā de yèwù ma?Dialogue 3 country tour — and there’s Lesson 4’s 具体 doing its job.
4新加坡对你们的糖业务来说，为什么重要？Xīnjiāpō duì nǐmen…wèishénme zhòngyào?GAP — needs a prepared answer: 总部, trading talent, 门户 to Asia.
5您以前不是做贸易的，为什么转换跑道？Nín yǐqián bú shì zuò màoyì de, wèishénme zhuǎnhuàn pǎodào?GAP — the career-arc question. Draft below.

Draft answer for Q5 — 草稿, for Queenie to correct

Deliberately built from patterns you already own (以前/后来/现在, 已经…了, 因为…所以, 跟…相关) plus this module’s 转换跑道 and 风险管理. It is a draft: bring it to class, let her fine-tune (微调), then it becomes Dialogue 4.
我的跑道也转换过。我以前是管理顾问，在贝恩公司工作。后来我加入丰益，负责集团的商业发展，比如并购和合资项目。之后，我负责糖业务的风险管理，已经十年了。现在我是糖业的负责人。对我来说，风险管理的经验很有用 —— 因为做贸易，最重要的就是管理风险。
Wǒ de pǎodào yě zhuǎnhuàn guo. Wǒ yǐqián shì guǎnlǐ gùwèn, zài Bèi’ēn Gōngsī gōngzuò. Hòulái wǒ jiārù Fēngyì, fùzé jítuán de shāngyè fāzhǎn, bǐrú bìnggòu hé hézī xiàngmù. Zhīhòu, wǒ fùzé táng yèwù de fēngxiǎn guǎnlǐ, yǐjīng shí nián le. Xiànzài wǒ shì tángyè de fùzérén. Duì wǒ lái shuō, fēngxiǎn guǎnlǐ de jīngyàn hěn yǒuyòng — yīnwèi zuò màoyì, zuì zhòngyào de jiù shì guǎnlǐ fēngxiǎn.
My track has switched too. I was a management consultant at Bain. Later I joined Wilmar, leading group business development — M&A and JV projects. After that I ran risk management for the sugar business, for ten years now. Today I head the sugar business. For me the risk experience is invaluable — because in trading, the most important thing is managing risk.
Spoken-delivery check before class: the 贸易/交易 boundary from Lesson 3 holds here — 做贸易 (the business), 管理风险 (the job). One deliberate echo: 最重要的就是 reuses the 就 clincher from 这就达成了一体化.

Read-aloud paragraph — the rung above the ladder

One paragraph per lesson, cold: reading aloud under novel-vocabulary load is a different skill from rehearsed scripts. Start with the 环环相扣 paragraph (both chunked sentences above, joined). Mark every 占-class hazard before you start: 供应链, 运转, 终端.
这些环环相扣，协助集团确保基本粮食和农业供应链的运转，把产品从世界各地的农民那里，运送到终端消费者手中。
Zhèxiē huánhuán xiāngkòu, xiézhù jítuán quèbǎo jīběn liángshi hé nóngyè gōngyìngliàn de yùnzhuǎn, bǎ chǎnpǐn cóng shìjiè gèdì de nóngmín nàli, yùnsòng dào zhōngduān xiāofèizhě shǒu zhōng.
These interlock link by link, helping the group ensure the basic food and agricultural supply chain keeps running — moving products from farmers across the world into the hands of end consumers.
